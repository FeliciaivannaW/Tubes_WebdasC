MAIN DISHES
Stargazy Pie
Stargazy pie originates from Mousehole, a small fishing village in Cornwall, England. It is linked to a local legend about a fisherman named Tom Bawcock, who went out to sea during a storm to save the village from starvation. To celebrate his bravery, a pie was made with fish heads poking through the crust to show that it was filled with fish and it became known as Stargazy Pie.

Toad in the Hole
Toad in the Hole is a traditional British dish made by baking sausages in a batter similar to Yorkshire pudding. The sausages are placed in a hot baking dish, and the batter is poured over them before baking. It's usually served with onion gravy and vegetables.

Yorkshire Pudding
Yorkshire pudding is a traditional British dish made from a batter of eggs, flour, and milk or water. It's baked in the oven until it puffs up and turns golden brown, with a crispy outside and soft, airy inside. Yorkshire pudding is most commonly served as part of a Sunday roast, especially with roast beef and gravy.

Fish and Chips
Fish and chips is one of the most iconic traditional British dishes. It consists of battered and deep-fried white fish (usually cod or haddock) served with thick-cut fried potatoes, known as chips. It's often enjoyed with sides like mushy peas, tartar sauce, or a splash of vinegar.
MAIN DISHES
Cornish Pasty
Cornish pasty is a traditional baked pastry from Cornwall, a region in the southwest of England. It's shaped like a half-moon and filled with beef, potatoes, onions, and swede (a type of turnip), all seasoned and wrapped in a golden, flaky crust.
MAIN DISHES
Beef Wellington
Beef Wellington is a classic British dish made of a beef tenderloin (usually filet mignon) coated with mushroom duxelles and sometimes pâté, then wrapped in puff pastry and baked until golden. The result is a crispy outside with tender, juicy beef inside.
MAIN DISHES
Steak and KidneyPie
Steak and kidney pie is a traditional British savory pie made with diced beef, kidney (usually lamb or pork), onions, and rich brown gravy, all encased in a flaky or shortcrust pastry.
MAIN DISHES
Lancashire Hotpot
Lancashire hotpot is a classic British stew made with tender pieces of lamb or mutton, sliced onions, and sometimes root vegetables like carrots. The ingredients are slow-cooked together and topped with thinly sliced potatoes arranged in layers, which turn golden and crispy as they bake. The result is a warm, hearty dish that's rich in flavor and perfect for colder days.
